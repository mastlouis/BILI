Visible and Invisible:

Examining Identitites

About our Spring Retreat!

To what extent do you feel like your religious/philosophical identities/worldview are visible in your day-to-day life? How often do you think about it, and in what circumstances? How do your identities, or your relationship to your identities, impact the way that you approach others and understand yourself? We will explore these questions and more at our annual retreat!

Where & When: The retreat is the weekend of February 22nd and February 23rd, 2020 at the MIT Endicott House in Deadham, Massachusetts.

How to get there: We will coordinate carpooling. Attendees are encouraged to use ride shares.

BILI is supported by a grant from the Arthur Vining Davis Foundations.
